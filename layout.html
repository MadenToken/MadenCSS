📦 .container

Centers the content within the page and limits its maximum width to 1600px for better readability and alignment.

📏 .row

Defines a horizontal flex container. Its child elements — typically columns — are arranged side by side using Flexbox.

🧱 .col Classes

This is a fixed 12-column layout system that does not respond to screen size. It is useful when you want columns to maintain a specific width, even on smaller screens.
